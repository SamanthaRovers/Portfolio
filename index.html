Miko Character Sheet

I designed each of the three characters following both shape theory and colour psychology. Miko is represented by the circle and the colour red. In focusing on rounded shapes and curved lines, Miko's pure and friendly personality shines through. Miko's primary colour is red as it is the colour of the traditional Japanese shrine maiden uniform. In Japan, the colour red represents strength, passion, and self sacrifice. Miko shows these qualities through her resolution to help everyone she meets even if it negatively impacts her, such as becoming possessed.

Made in Procreate

Gou Character Sheet

Gou is represented by the square and the colour blue. Gou has played the role of warrior, husband, father, and most recently protector. All of which require strength, stability, and reliability. I designed Gou focusing on boxy shapes to emphasize those traits. Gou's primary colour is blue. Blue represents his loyalty, trustworthiness, confidence, and wisdom.

Made in Procreate

Shun Character Sheet

Shun is represented by the triangle and the colour green. Shun, like the triangle is exciting, energetic, and perhaps even a little dangerous. When designing Shun I used many triangular shapes and sharp angles to show those traits. Green represents nature and movement as well as prosperity and growth. Both themes represent Shun well. Shun is often seen moving from town to town trying to acquire items he can sell. He wishes to shed his thieving past and become a successful and legitimate merchant.
